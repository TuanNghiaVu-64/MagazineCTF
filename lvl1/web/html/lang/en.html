What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the creation of systems that can perform tasks requiring human intelligence, like learning, reasoning, and decision-making. It’s not just robots—AI powers things like voice assistants, recommendation engines, and even self-driving cars. AI includes areas like Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Computer Vision. These technologies help machines understand data, learn from it, and improve over time. In essence, AI helps automate tasks, solve problems, and enhance how we interact with technology every day.
